What Is Ceramic Coating?

Protection That Bonds to Your Paint

Ceramic coating is a liquid polymer that chemically bonds to your vehicle's paint, creating a permanent protective layer that wax and sealants simply cannot match. Once cured, it forms a hard, hydrophobic surface that repels water, dirt, and contaminants with ease.

Unlike wax that washes away in weeks, a properly applied ceramic coating lasts 2–7+ years depending on the product tier — and makes every wash dramatically easier.

✓
Hydrophobic Beading

✓
UV & Oxidation Protection

✓

Protects against bird droppings, tree sap, road salt, and harsh chemicals that damage clear coat.

✓
Easier Maintenance
