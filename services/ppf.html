What Is PPF?

The Clear Shield Your Paint Needs

Paint Protection Film (PPF) — also called clear bra — is a virtually invisible urethane film applied directly to your vehicle's painted surfaces. It absorbs the impact of road debris, rock chips, and minor scratches before they ever reach your paint.

Modern PPF is self-healing: minor surface scratches disappear with heat from the sun or a heat gun. Once the film's useful life is done (typically 10+ years), it can be removed cleanly without damaging the underlying paint.
